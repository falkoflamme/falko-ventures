A treasure hunt built on your team's real knowledge.

Guests receive a unique QR code at check-in. Insider Keys opens in their browser — no app download. They see a city map with hidden spots, chosen personally by your team.

As they walk toward a spot, their phone becomes a compass. The distance counts down live. When they arrive, the phone vibrates and the staff member's voice plays automatically.

The Technology

Every capability of the
modern smartphone. No app.

Everything runs in the mobile browser. No download. No friction. Just scan and explore.

Live Compass Navigation

DeviceOrientation API turns the phone into a real compass. The arrow rotates in real time pointing toward the next hidden spot. Distance counts down live.

📳

Proximity Haptics

The Vibration API pulses the phone as guests approach. At 80m: one pulse. At 20m: three pulses — arrival signal. No need to look at the screen.

80m — proximity pulse
20m — arrival signal

Staff Voice on Arrival

Web Audio API plays a 20-second voice note — recorded by the actual staff member — the moment the guest arrives. Her voice. Her words. Her place.
